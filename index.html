Designing Interactions with GenAI for Art and Creativity

Welcome to the project page for our systematic review and taxonomy on designing interactions with Generative AI (GenAI) for art and creativity. This research delves into how GenAI systems are transforming the landscape of artistic and creative expression, enabling more intuitive and user-centered design interactions.

Introduction

Generative AI (GenAI) technologies, such as Large Language Models (LLMs) and Vision-Language Models (VLMs), have begun to reshape the field of art and creativity by offering new ways to generate and interact with creative content. However, despite the growing interest in these systems, there is a lack of a comprehensive framework that addresses the design of interactive GenAI systems specifically for artistic domains. Our review aims to fill this gap by presenting a structured taxonomy for designing user interactions with GenAI in the context of art and creativity. This work not only synthesizes existing approaches but also proposes new directions for future research, focusing on how these systems can better support creative professionals and enthusiasts.

In this page, you can download the Interaction Design Resources Slide Deck, which encapsulates key insights from our paper, offering practical guidelines for those looking to design or work with GenAI systems in creative fields.
